Jumpstarts

Action-oriented video prompts that give writers a formalized structure to engage with their ideas

Watch

Jumpstarts are packaged as video prompts that you can watch anywhere, anytime. Follow along with the exercises and examples on your phone, desktop, laptop, or tablet.

Write

Follow along with the Jumpstart exercises to engage with your ideas. The prompts are designed to get you thinking about your writing in a quick and productive manner.

Think

Jumpstart prompts have all been professionally crafted to get you thinking about your writing. A quick Jumpstart is all you need to organize your thoughts and overcome any mental blockers.

What is a Jumpstart?

Jumpstarts are writing exercises designed to be engaging and productive in a short period of time.
